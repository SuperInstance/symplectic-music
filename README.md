# symplectic-music

**Hamiltonian mechanics for musical harmony** — treat chord progressions as Hamiltonian flows in phase space, with energy conservation governing voice leading.

## What This Gives You

- **Symplectic maps** — chord progressions as area-preserving transformations
- **Hamiltonian flows** — voice leading governed by conserved quantities
- **Phase space analysis** — visualize harmonic trajectories in musical phase space
- **Energy conservation** — musical "energy" (tension) conserved under symplectic evolution

## How It Fits

Part of the SuperInstance music-mathematics research. Connects to `counterpoint-engine` (constraint-based counterpoint) and `holonomy-harmony-rs` (holonomy in harmony).
